About

I have recently graduated with a Bachelor of Visual Communications from Christchurch Polytechnic Institute of Technology (CPIT). I first discovered design by accident, I was interested in film but unfortunately there weren’t enough students at my high school to make up a class, so I was asked to pick another subject, which was Digital Art Design. My background is in Photo manipulation and I still enjoy it today. I first started experimenting with Photoshop at 16 and wanted to become more skilled, I have since then progressed this through my studies.

Through online tutorials and teaching myself I was proud of the outcomes I was producing. So I decided that was what I wanted to do and I started my Bachelor of Design degree three years ago. Ever since then I’ve been constantly striving to improve my skills. Design excites me; everything in my life is affected by it from buying stationery to the camera I decide to purchase. Design is not as easy as it looks; producing the work isn’t the hardest it’s the thought behind it.
